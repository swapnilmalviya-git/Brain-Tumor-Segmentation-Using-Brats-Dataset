To train Unet-3D model:
python3 code/train-Unet.py

To train ResUnet-3D model:
python3 code/train-ResUnet.py

To train Unet-2D model:
python3 code/train_unet2d.py

To train ResUnet-2D model:
python3 code/train_ResUnet_2d.py


The dataset can be downloaded from:
http://braintumorsegmentation.org/


The 'data' folder contains the dummy data with only two MRI images of 4 modalities each.
